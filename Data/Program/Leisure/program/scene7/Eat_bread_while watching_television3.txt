Eat bread while watching television3
Find TV in the living room to be a good way to wind down from the stress of everyday life. Turn on the TV. Grab a remote to find a program where we can turn the brain off and enjoy the laughs. Walk over to the bread slice in the kitchen. Grab the bread slice and walks back to the living room. Then, sit on the sofa and eat bread slice while watching TV. Turn off the TV after watching TV programs.


[WALK] <tv> (1)
[SWITCHON] <tv> (1)
[WALK] <remotecontrol> (1)
[GRAB] <remotecontrol> (1)
[WALK] <kitchen> (1)
[WALK] <breadslice> (1)
[GRAB] <breadslice> (1)
[WALK] <livingroom> (1)
[WALK] <sofa> (1)
[SIT] <sofa> (1)
[EAT] <breadslice> (1)
[STAND]
[WALK] <tv> (1)
[WALK] <tvstand> (1)
[PUTBACK] <remotecontrol> (1) <tvstand> (1)
[SWITCHOFF] <tv> (1)
